Property Styling Automation

End-to-end workflow automation for Aggie's property styling business, from lead capture and quoting through to job completion and client follow-up.

The Problem We Are Solving
Too Much Admin, Not Enough Time for the Work That Matters
Right now, every job runs on memory, emails, and a dated supplier spreadsheet. You visit the property, work out what is needed, then manually price the job using Pabs' hire list, calculate your own markup separately, and put together a quote by hand. Client details live in emails. Invoices are built from a Canva template one at a time. There is no single place to see which jobs are quoted, which are paid, and which are coming up. Follow-ups, reminders, and collection dates all depend on you remembering to do them. When you are physically moving furniture, making beds, and styling three properties a week, that admin piles up fast. This system removes the repetitive work entirely. You fill in one form on your phone at the property, and everything else, the quote, the hire agreement, the invoice, the scheduling, the client communication, and the follow-up, happens automatically. Less time at your desk at 3am, more capacity to take on the work you actually enjoy.
